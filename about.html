Our story

Built for Gaborone

Finding trustworthy help for home jobs in Botswana has always been word-of-mouth. Handled changes that — by making it easy to post a task, find a verified worker, and complete a job with full accountability on both sides.

🛡️

Trust first

Every worker is KYC-verified before they can take any job.

💬

Transparency

Prices are fixed and shown before you post. No surprises.

📸

Accountability

Before and after photos are mandatory on every job.

🤝

Fair for workers

Workers set their availability and keep 85% of every job.

Trust & Safety

What happens when something goes wrong

Contact support within 48 hours

Email or WhatsApp us within 48 hours of the job being marked done.

We review the photos

Both the before and after photos are reviewed by our team to assess the situation.

Mediation & resolution

We contact both parties and work toward a fair outcome. Refunds are assessed case by case.

Escalation

If unresolved, the case may be referred to a third party or relevant authority.
